Finmacadmin 2014.2

03 Nov 2014

The second Finmacdmin-meeting was planned at MacSysadmin 2014 in Gothenburg, and mainly arranged by Robin Laurén at Reaktor.

We managed to have several very interesting presentations:

  • Joel Manserus from mcare talked about the AppleCare products
  • Hannes Juutilainen built a local Munki system in just under 15 minutes
  • Janne Lehikoinen from Tieto talked about git in systems administration
